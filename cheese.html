Grilled Cheese

Description:

Grilled cheese is a classic American sandwich that is usually prepared with one
or more cheese varieties that are placed between two slices of bread.
The slices are buttered from the outside, and the sandwich is then
shortly heated, typically in a pan or on a griddle, until the bread
is golden and the cheese melts.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • slices Cheddar cheese
